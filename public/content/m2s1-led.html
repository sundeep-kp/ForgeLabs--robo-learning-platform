Module 2, Session 1: LED Basics

What You'll Learn

This session introduces the basics of using LEDs in electronics projects. You'll learn how to safely connect and control LEDs with Arduino.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Identify the anode and cathode of an LED.
  2. Connect the LED to Arduino using a current-limiting resistor.
  3. Upload a simple blink sketch to test your setup.
