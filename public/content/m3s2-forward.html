Module 3, Session 2: Forward Kinematics

What You'll Learn

This session introduces forward kinematics, the process of determining the position of a robot's end effector from its joint angles.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Review the concept of kinematics in robotics.
  2. Learn the equations for forward kinematics of a simple arm.
  3. Apply these equations to calculate the position of the end effector.
