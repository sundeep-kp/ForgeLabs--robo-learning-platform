Module 3, Session 1: Torque

What You'll Learn

This session explains the concept of torque and its importance in robotics. You'll learn how to calculate and select motors based on torque requirements.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Understand the definition of torque and its units (Newton-meters).
  2. Learn how torque affects robotic arm movement and payload.
  3. Calculate required torque for a simple robotic joint.
